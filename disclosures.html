About & disclosures

This is a small, independent study tool one student built for their own class. A few things worth knowing before you use it.

Not an official course resource

Independent, unofficial project

This site isn't affiliated with, reviewed by, or endorsed by your school, program, or instructors. It's a personal study aid built from one student's own notes. Treat it as a supplement, not a replacement for assigned readings, lectures, or your instructor's guidance — and always defer to your official course materials if anything here conflicts with them.

AI-generated content

The AI chat and Teach Back grading can be wrong

Responses from the AI chat and Teach Back feedback are generated by a language model. Like any AI, it can occasionally misstate a fact, oversimplify a mechanism, or miss something important — especially in a field like pathophysiology where precision matters. Cross-check anything you're unsure about against your textbook or instructor, and never use this site as a substitute for clinical judgment or patient-care decisions.

Voice feature

Microphone input uses your browser's speech recognition

The microphone button uses your browser's built-in speech-to-text (typically Chrome's Web Speech API). Depending on your browser, audio may be sent to your browser vendor's servers (e.g. Google) for transcription — it does not pass through this site's own servers. Spoken replies (text-to-speech) are generated locally on your device. If you'd rather not have audio leave your device at all, just type your question instead of using the mic.

Payments & data

What's collected, and by whom

  • Payments are processed entirely by Stripe. This site never sees or stores your card number — Stripe collects your email and payment details directly.
  • This site stores a Stripe customer reference and your remaining AI credit balance in your own browser's local storage — nothing is tracked across devices or shared with anyone else.
  • Questions you type into the AI chat or Teach Back are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a response, the same way any AI chat tool works. They aren't stored by this site afterward.
  • Purchases are one-time and non-refundable once credits are added to your balance, since usage can't be undone. If a charge genuinely went wrong (duplicate charge, no credits received, etc.), contact the site owner directly to sort it out.
Availability

Run by a student, not a company

This site is hosted on free/low-cost infrastructure and maintained by one person alongside their coursework. There's no uptime guarantee, and features may change or occasionally break. It's provided as-is, in the spirit of helping classmates study.
