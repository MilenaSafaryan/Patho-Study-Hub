Patho Study Hub is a self-study companion built for our Pathophysiology course, organized to match the syllabus week by week. For every chapter, you get:

A study guide with the original mind-map diagrams, comparison tables, and key concepts
An auto-generated quiz pulled straight from the chapter's glossary
Flashcards to flip through term by term
A Teach Back mode that checks your own explanation against the real definitions, one topic at a time
An optional AI Tutor that explains concepts simply and asks comprehension questions as you go

There's also a full searchable glossary (organized by chapter), practice exams with real multiple-choice questions for each week, and a Final Exam Prep tool that mixes questions from every week together with a timer and a breakdown of your weakest areas.

It's free to use — the only paid part is the AI features (chat and Tutor), which use a small prepaid credit system just to cover the cost of running them.
